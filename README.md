# pymaceuticals_challenge
In this challenge, I analyzed data of the effects of anti-cancer medications tested on mice. The data includes information on 249 mice who were identified with SCC tumors received treatment with a range of drug regimens. Over the course of 45 days, tumor development was observed and measured.
